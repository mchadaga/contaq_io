Frequently Asked Questions


What's included in the output?

- Company Website, Contact Name, Contact Title, Contact LinkedIn URL
- Double-Verified Email of Contact

Who is this for?

You sell B2B software or services to e-commerce stores
Your company has a strong, validated offer

Who is this NOT for?

Your company doesn't have a proven offer yet
You blast 1000s of unpersonalized emails to potential clients (spray-and-pray approach)
You need revenue data, technographic data, or intent data

How do credits work?

When you subscribe, you get a certain number of credits per month. Your account will be charged 1 credit per verified email you scrape. You can specify the maximum number of credits you want to spend on a single scrape. Credits rollover to following months if you remain subscribed.

What are double-verified emails?

To ensure you get as few bounces as possible, we run every email through two highly-reputable email verifiers: AnyMailFinder and NeverBounce. If an email does not succeed both verifications, you won't be charged for it and you won't even know it existed.

What does verifying an email even mean?

It means to create a connection with an email server to check if a specific address exists and can accept emails. Certain servers are "catchall," meaning they will always accept emails, so if an email is flagged as "catchall" we will discard it because these aren't *really* verified.

Where does Contaq.io scrape the data from?

Google Search, Google Shopping, Google Places, and LinkedIn

Do I need to download any proxies, scraping software, or Chrome extensions?

Nope!

Do I need to stay on the page while the scrape runs?

Nope. Most scrapes take 10-20 minutes to run, and we will email you when the scrape is complete.

What's the refund policy?

Full refund if Contaq.io doesn't work as intended.
